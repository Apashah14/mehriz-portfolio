01 — Project Overview

Designing the future of luxury homeware retail in Iran

E-Commerce RTL/LTR Figma Mobile Desktop Pip Studio

Mehriz Home is a pioneering multi-brand platform in Iran, representing several premium international homeware brands, including Pip Studio Amsterdam. The main design challenge was to create a digital shopping experience that showcases multiple luxury brands while remaining intuitive for users, especially since multi-brand e-commerce is relatively new in the Iranian market.

From a UX perspective, one of the key challenges was the client’s complex navigation: there were too many subcategories in the menu, which risked overwhelming users. To tackle this, I conducted user surveys, interviews, and A/B testing to design a navigation structure that satisfies all the client’s organizational needs while making it simple for users to find what they’re looking for.

The project covers a full e-commerce ecosystem, including landing pages, product listings, product detail pages, basket and checkout flows, wedding registry, gift cards, and an “About” section, all designed to reflect the premium positioning of the brands while ensuring a seamless experience in both Persian (RTL) and English (LTR), across mobile and desktop.

06 — Design Process

How the design came together

01
Discovery & Brand

Understanding the Pip Studio brand values, the luxury homeware market in Iran, and the bilingual audience requirements.

02
Information Architecture

Mapping all user flows: browse → product → basket → checkout, plus registry creation, gift card purchase, and account management.

03
Visual Design

Multiple rounds of refinement — from raw explorations to final polished screens with micro-interactions and edge case handling.

07 — Key Features Designed
E-Commerce Flow

Complete product discovery, listing filters, detailed product pages, basket management, and multi-step checkout with progress indicators.

Wedding Registry

Full registry creation and sharing flow — guests can browse, purchase gifts, and manage their contribution to a couple's wishlist.

Gift Card System

Multiple gift card denominations in 4 color variants (BGC, RGC, YGC, GGC) with credit states 0–8 designed systematically.

Bilingual UI

Every screen designed in both Farsi (RTL, Vazir font) and English (LTR, Roboto), with mirrored layouts and culturally appropriate typography.

Mobile-First Design

375px mobile screens with native-feel gestures: horizontal scroll for product carousels, bottom sheet modals, thumb-zone CTAs.

Mega Navigation

Desktop mega-menu with categorized product navigation (dining, bath, bedroom, lighting, decor) featuring beautiful lifestyle imagery.
